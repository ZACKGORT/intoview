Core flow (for reference)
1) Capture or Upload → 2) New Note Sheet → 3) Choose Ratio → 4) Save → 5) Card appears in Library → 6) Export with film-strip chrome.
• Title max 40 chars
• Note max 100 chars (required)
• Location autofills Title + top strip if allowed
• Timestamp always shows in bottom strip
• Burst = press & hold
• Ratio chips = 1:1 or 4:3

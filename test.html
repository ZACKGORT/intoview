Reclaim the Em Dash — A Manifesto

In the digital age, where words race across screens and attention spans shrink, the em dash— that elegant, emphatic pause— has been marginalized, reduced to a mere hyphen or buried in autocorrect's indifference. We call upon writers, thinkers, and creators to reclaim this punctuation of precision and power.

The Power of the Em Dash

The em dash interrupts with intention— it connects ideas, adds emphasis, and breathes life into prose. Unlike the timid hyphen or the rigid period, it maintains the distortion effect, bending language to reflect the complexity of thought.

The Distortion in Modern Society

Today's keyboards and algorithms favor speed over subtlety— the em dash is often replaced, forgotten, or ignored. This distortion erodes expressive writing, flattening our communication in an era that demands nuance.

Call to Action
